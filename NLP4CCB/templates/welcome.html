About This Game

Welcome!

First, create an account if you don't have one already, and sign in.
Click Start Round to begin playing a game.
For each round, you'll have 20 seconds to identify as many word relations as you can.

Example: If the relationship is meronym, you may be asked to "Name parts of a face?"
To which you might answer...
  • eyes
  • nose
  • ears
  • mouth
  • chin
Your score for each word is a percentage of how many other players have also said it!
Bonus points (50) are awarded for words in WordNet, a handcrafted lexical database.
Repeated words or empty boxes will not be considered for scoring.
Finally, use tab or enter to create another box for word entry.

Enjoy the game!
