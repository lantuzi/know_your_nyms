About this game

WELCOME!
This game is designed to solve the problem of semantic relationship discovery.
Create an account if you don't have one already.
Once you signup/login, you will be prompted to choose a type of word relation.
You can also choose the number of rounds you'd like to play
Then, click Start playing to begin the timer.
For each round, you'll have 10, 15, or 20 seconds to identify as many relations as you can.

For example,

If the relationship is meronym, you may be asked

"What are parts of a face?"

to which you might answer...

eyes
nose
mouth
eyebrows
etc...

Once the timer runs out, the round is finished.
After your last round, you'll see a performance summary
